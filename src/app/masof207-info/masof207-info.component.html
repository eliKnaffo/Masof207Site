Terminal 207 specializes in giving logistic solutions for any kind of cargo(before customs).
From the moment your cargo leaves the ship and until it reaches the shelf or its final destination.
Our unique equipment and methods allow for fast load and unloading using advanced information systems.
Our customers can monitor their cargo advancement through our system in real time through our website and app.
Terminal207 was established in 1996, it is located in Israel sea port park in Ashdod.
The terminal is ISO 9001:2008 certification approved by the israeli customs.
